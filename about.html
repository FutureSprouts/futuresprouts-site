About us

Planting knowledge. Growing impact.

FutureSprouts is a federally recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization that empowers students and communities through hands-on farming education, seed sharing, and environmental stewardship that creates lasting, measurable impact.

Our Mission

Teaching communities to grow food while growing themselves.

FutureSprouts brings farming and environmental education to life for students, families, and communities. Our garden programs go beyond the classroom by building real skills, deepening our connection to food, and inspiring a lifelong respect for the earth. We believe that when people grow things, they grow too.

Education

Learn By Growing

Workshops and hands-on garden sessions that teach students how food is grown, how soil works, and why what we eat matters.

Access

Tools To Thrive

Seeds, gloves, and garden supplies made available to students and classrooms so every kid can participate regardless of background.

Stewardship

Growing Responsibility

Garden projects and environmental programs that show students how to care for the earth through real, meaningful action.
